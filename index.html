General Heading Creation

In Ontario, all court documents begin with a general heading. This heading identifies the court, the file number, and the parties involved. Once created it will apply to all documents in your case, including affidavits. Please provide the following information:

Court Information - -
Plaintiff(s) Information

Enter the first plaintiff (the first name listed on your Statement of Claim or Notice of Application).

If the plaintiff is an individual, use First and Last Name. If it is a company, leave the person fields blank and enter the full legal company name.

– OR –
Defendant(s) Information

Enter the first defendant (the first name listed on your Statement of Claim or Notice of Application).

If the defendant is an individual, use First and Last Name.

If the defendant is a company, leave the person fields blank and enter the full legal company name.
